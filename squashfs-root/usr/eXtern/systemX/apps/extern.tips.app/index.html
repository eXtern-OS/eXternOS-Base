Getting Started

Here are some tips and tricks that will help you take full advantage of eXtern OS

Touchpad Gestures

Slide three fingers from left to right and/or right to left to switch between workspaces

You can also slide three fingers bottom to top and/or top to bottom to show or hide all workspaces
